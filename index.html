ENDÜSTRİ

Cemiyette Pişiyorum · Punk Rock · Release Date: October 10, 2025

The band's brand new studio album ENDÜSTRİ is out now!

Gallery Promo Image (HQ) Spotify Apple Music YouTube Music

ENDÜSTRİ is the seventh studio album by Istanbul-based duo Cemiyette Pişiyorum.

Prior to the album release, "Aşk ve Anarşi" and "Cennet" were released as singles.

The album was recorded at Maslak 1024 / Istanbul by Murat Gülbay.

Album Concept

The core themes of ENDÜSTRİ revolve around isolation, social inequality, and marginalization. These concepts are deeply explored through both the lyrics and the musical narrative.
